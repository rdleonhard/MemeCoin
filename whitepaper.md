Uniswap V2 is a decentralized trading protocol on the Ethereum blockchain. It allows for the exchange of any two ERC20 tokens without requiring a centralized intermediary. Uniswap uses an automated liquidity protocol, meaning that the platform itself does not create orders or execute trades. Instead, these functions are performed by smart contracts and liquidity pools that hold balances of two tokens and adjust pricing between them using a formula.
