# 1. Introduction

## 1.1. Purpose of the White Paper

This white paper serves as a comprehensive guide to **Meme Coin**, a unique cryptocurrency token adhering to the ERC20 standard, and its intended one-time issuance on Uniswap V2. By reading this white paper, interested parties will gain a thorough understanding of Meme Coin's technical specifications, the process of its listing on Uniswap V2, and the issuers' unique hands-off approach post-issuance. The objective is to provide all necessary information to potential investors, regulators, and the broader crypto community, ensuring transparency and clarity regarding Meme Coin's specifications and future.

## 1.2. Overview of the Meme Coin

**Meme Coin** is an ERC20 token with a limited supply. Born out of the evolving cryptocurrency landscape, Meme Coin embodies the spirit of the decentralized economy. The token will be listed on Uniswap V2 with an Ethereum (ETH) pairing, allowing anyone to purchase and trade it.

Unlike many other tokens, the issuers of Meme Coin will have no further involvement with its development following its issuance. This hands-off approach represents a paradigm shift in the cryptocurrency landscape, where the future of Meme Coin will be entirely determined by the community and the open market. This white paper outlines the technical aspects, issuance process, and implications of this unique approach.
